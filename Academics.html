COURSES OFFERED

SCHOOL OF INFORMATION SCIENCES

With a reputation as a centre for learning, over the two decades, the school since 1996 has provided sound knowledge among the students in the areas of computer science, computer applications and information technology. The vision of the school is to emerge as a centre disseminating and moulding technically competent computer professionals for national building. The school organizes conduct various competitions in the inter/intra levels. It also conducts workshop, Industrial visits Seminar in association with industry experts and associations. The students are being taught by experienced faculty members and extensive practical knowledge is being imparted to face challenges in the IT field and to meet the global competition too.

SCHOOL OF BIOSCIENCES

The school of biosciences comprising of UG and PG biotechnology since 2002 provides students with a unique, multidisciplinary and collaborative learning experience to make them technicians, researchers and entrepreneurs. The school has been extraordinary in organizing hands on training, workshops and conferences on need of the hour areas. The PG biotechnology specializes in doing research on medical biotechnology, Green Nanotechnology, application oriented technology and UG biotechnology on Algology.
