SPORTS

Sports is a physical activity that involves competition between individuals or teams. It requires physical strength, endurance, agility, and mental focus. Kumararani Meena Muthiah Arts and Science College is a renowned educational institution that offers various courses in arts, science, and management. The college also has a vibrant sports culture and encourages students to participate in various sports and games. The college has a well-equipped sports complex that provides facilities for various sports like basketball, volleyball, badminton, cricket, football, and athletics. T he college also has a well-trained team of coaches who provide training and guidance to the students. Kumararani Meena Muthiah Arts and Science College has produced many talented sportspeople who have excelled in various sports and brought laurels to the institution

CULTURALS

Culturals Festival

Culture can be defined as the beliefs, customs, and artistic expressions of a particular group of people. Kumararani Meena Muthiah Arts and Science College is an educational institution located in Chennai, India that places a strong emphasis on promoting and preserving Indian culture. The college offers a wide range of cultural activities and programs for its students, such as classical dance and music performances, theatre productions, and traditional art exhibitions. These events showcase the rich and diverse cultural heritage of India and help students to develop a deeper appreciation and understanding of their own cultural roots.

NSS-NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is an initiative by the Government of India to engage students in community service activities. Kumararani Meena Muthiah College (KRMCC) also has an active NSS unit that organizes various activities for students to participate in. These activities include blood donation camps, tree plantation drives, awareness campaigns on social issues, and volunteering for community development programs..

YOUTH RED CROSS

YRC is a youth organization active in KRMMC. Its main objective is to develop social service spirit in young people. YRC members participate in blood donation camps, health awareness and disaster relief initiatives. YRC provides a platform for developing leadership skills through community service. It is a great opportunity for students to make a positive impact on society and develop personal and professional skills.
