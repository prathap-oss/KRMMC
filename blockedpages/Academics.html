About the Department


The department of was established during the year 2000. BCA shift 2 was launched during 2011-12. The BCA degree prepares the students for their job requirements of the fast developing IT/Software industry. The department conducts orientation to make students feel at ease with the content of the degree curriculum and to develop rapport with their peers. ICONS- Students of BCA organises cultural activities, cloud activities, fests, seminars, workshops, conferences, for their all-round development.

Highlights of the department


  • A rich equipped department library, electronic gadgets like LCD Projector and Broadband facility, for enhanced teaching- learning.
  • Various add-on certificate courses are conducted to make the student computer literate, self-sufficient and accomplished as per the requirement of the present scenario.
  • Alumni Interaction, Guest lectures, Industrial visits.
  • Technical & Non-technical augmentation program to enhance the IT & soft skills.
  • Motivating the interested students in core Software Development
  • Excellent results with University ranks.
