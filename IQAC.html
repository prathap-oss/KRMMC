About - KRMMC -IQAC

To march towards academic excellence and ensure quality at all levels, Internal Quality Assurance Cell was established in the college on 13th J uly 2006 during the Post NAAC period. Since its inception, IQAC has been steadily working as a facilitating agent among all the stakeholders of education focusing on the goal of enhancing and sustaining quality. The prime responsibility of IQAC has been to initiate, plan, and execute various activities that make a significant impact on the quality of education imparted by the institution. IQAC is continuously engaged in channelizing all the efforts of the institution towards promoting quality as a culture and holistic academic excellence. It focuses on creating learner-centric environment and developing quality benchmarks for various academic activities

OBJECTIVE

To develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution

Benefits IQAC will facilitate / contribute to

  • Ensure clarity and focus in institutional functioning towards quality enhancement;
  • Ensure internalization of the quality culture;
  • Ensure enhancement and coordination among various activities of the institution and institutionalize all good practices;
  • Provide a sound basis for decision-making to improve institutional functioning; and organizing a good documentation process

GENERAL INITIATIVES TOWARDS INTERNALISING QUALITY @ KRMMC

  • Prepare annual plan of the year and also follow up the action taken for each plan
  • Review on teaching – learning process, learning outcome and methodology –curriculum enrichment and delivery, Mentor system, program and course outcome, Student Satisfaction Survey.
  • Review and collection of feedback from all the stake holders
  • Evaluate the programs, seminars and conferences and suggest recommendations wherever necessary.
  • Participation in NIRF
  • Disseminate the information on changing higher education scenario and sustenance of quality to all the stake holders
